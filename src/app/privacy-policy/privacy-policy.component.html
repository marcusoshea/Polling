Privacy Policy for AE Polling

Effective Date: May 21st, 2025

AE Polling ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the AE Polling Mobile application ("App"). AE Polling is a tool for participating in polling for various Orders within the Kingdom of Æthelmearc, which you must already be a member of, in conjunction with the website https://polling.aethelmearc.org.

By using this App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. User-Provided Information

To use AE Polling, you are required to create an account either within the app or at polling.aethelmearc.org. After your account has been approved, you may:

  • Select an Order to log into
  • Participate in polls
  • Store comments about candidates
  • Update your name, email, and password

This information is handled directly by the api-polling.aethelmearc.org website.

b. Automatically Collected Information

AE Polling does not collect or store personal information beyond the name and email address associated with your login credentials. All such data is managed by https://api-polling.aethelmearc.org/, which serves as the backend infrastructure for both the website and app.

2. How We Use Information

AE Polling does not independently collect, store, or process any personal or behavioral data, beyond temporary in-memory storage required to maintain your login session and name to attribute to your comments about candidates. This includes temporary session state and authentication tokens that are not saved to persistent storage. The app functions solely as a client interface to the Æthelmearc Polling system. All user data is managed by https://api-polling.aethelmearc.org/.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties. Any data submitted through the app is transmitted directly to https://api-polling.aethelmearc.org/ and is governed by the data management policies of that service.

4. Security

AE Polling follows standard Android security practices. All communication between the app and api-polling.aethelmearc.org is encrypted using secure protocols (HTTPS). The backend service is responsible for the encryption, protection, and storage of all user data submitted through both the app and website.

5. Children’s Privacy

AE Polling is intended for users 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you are under 18, please do not use this app or submit any personal data.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted within the app and reflected by the “Effective Date” above.
